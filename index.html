About

iBantu Technologies is a company conceived out of the need to turn individual, community & corporate discomfort, frustration or inconveniences into tangible solutions that can be scaled, profitably or for the greater good of humanity, in the spirit of "Ubuntu".

In essence, our goal is to utilize technology as a tool for the betterment of every human's day to day experience globally, in a sustainable manner, for as long as God Almighty allows us.

Although we are now a duly registered Private Company, under the company laws of Zimbabwe and are ZIMRA compliant, our mission and commitment to the use of technology for offering solutions to problems faced by those around us pre-dates our formal incoporation.
