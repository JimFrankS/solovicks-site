Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

iBantu Technologies ("we," "us," "our," or "company") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website, SaaS platforms, IoT solutions, and WhatsApp Business automation tools.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways:

  • Communication Data: Phone numbers, email addresses, and message content provided via our WhatsApp ChatBots and contact forms.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, device information, and IoT device telemetry data.
  • Registration Data: Information provided for company registration services, including identification details and business particulars.
  • Usage Data: Analytics about how you interact with our services and platforms.

3. Multi-Tenant & Customer Data

For customers that use our SaaS platforms, WhatsApp automation, or workflow services, we process communication and transaction data as a Data Processor on behalf of our business clients. Our business clients act as the Data Controllers, and they retain control over their end-users' data.

We do not own, control, or use customer communication payloads for our own independent purposes. We only process, transmit, and store that data to provide the licensed technical infrastructure services requested by our clients.

This includes routing business customer messages through Meta Platforms, Inc. APIs and supporting the execution of automation workflows under our clients’ instructions.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our technical services and solutions.
  • Facilitate company registrations and related administrative processes.
  • Monitor IoT system health and performance.
  • Ensure the cybersecurity and integrity of our infrastructure.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Comply with legal obligations under Zimbabwean law.
  • Process business customer communication and automation workflows on behalf of our clients.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal data or business communication payloads with third parties under any circumstances, except as required to provide our technical infrastructure services (for example, routing data through Meta Platforms, Inc. APIs) or as required by law.

5. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party service providers to deliver certain aspects of our services:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc.: Provides WhatsApp messaging infrastructure. Data processed through WhatsApp is subject to Meta's privacy standards and terms.
  • Cloud Infrastructure Providers: We use reputable cloud services for secure hosting and data management.

Where we process WhatsApp or other customer communication data, we do so only to provide infrastructure and routing services on behalf of our business clients, and we do not own or independently commercialize that data. This routing is limited to delivering the requested technical infrastructure services, and we do not use customer payloads for analytics, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to service delivery.

We ensure all third-party providers maintain adequate data protection standards consistent with the Data Protection Act of Zimbabwe [Chapter 11:12] and international best practices.

6. Data Security

As a technology and engineering company, we implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure authentication, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your data subject to legal and contractual requirements.

For our WhatsApp automation and workflow services, transaction and message payload data processed via webhooks is retained only for the duration required to execute the automation workflow and ensure delivery, or as explicitly configured by our business clients for their operational logs.

Additional business customer communication data may be retained only as required to fulfill contractual obligations, comply with applicable law, or support dispute resolution.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective upon posting to our website. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
