Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

iBantu Technologies ("we," "us," "our," or "company") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website, SaaS platforms, IoT solutions, and WhatsApp Business automation tools.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in several ways:

  • Communication Data: Phone numbers, email addresses, and message content provided via our WhatsApp ChatBots and contact forms.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, device information, and IoT device telemetry data.
  • Registration Data: Information provided for company registration services, including identification details and business particulars.
  • Usage Data: Analytics about how you interact with our services and platforms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our technical services and solutions.

  • Comply with legal obligations under Zimbabwean law.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

We ensure all third-party providers maintain adequate data protection standards consistent with Zimbabwe's data protection requirements.

5. Data Security

As a technology and engineering company, we implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your data subject to legal and contractual requirements.
